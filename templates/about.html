About us

Located in the lovely area of Tangalla Beach Front, Pastissade Beach Hotel enjoys a commanding position in the family fun, beaches, romance hub of Tangalle. The hotel is not too far from the city center: just 5 km away, and it normally takes about 210 minutes to reach the airport. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations. Take advantage of a wealth of unrivaled services and amenities at this Tangalle hotel. Guests of the hotel can enjoy on-site features like 24-hour room service, free Wi-Fi in all rooms, 24-hour security, taxi service, shrine. The hotel features 5 beautifully appointed guest rooms, many of which include internet access – wireless, desk, balcony/terrace, blackout curtains, fan. Throughout the day you can enjoy the relaxing atmosphere of the snorkeling, surfing lessons, private beach, diving, fishing. Pastissade Beach Hotel is your one-stop destination for quality hotel accommodations in Tangalle.
